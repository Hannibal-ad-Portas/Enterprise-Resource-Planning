Nebula:

Subscription Based ERP


Nebula is an online Enterprise Resource Planning platform with a subscription based model built for efficiency and designed for affordability

With the Nebula platform any organization from a non-profit to small businesses to large enterprises can have the same resource management at affordable prices





About


Nebula is a school project for CSC 380 at The Catholic University Of America in Washington D.C.

Pricing

These are the subscription tiers for the Nebula service:

Free
for companies with fewer than 50 employees
  • - Up to 50 Employees
  • - Less than $100k in yearly revenue
  • - Ideal for small businesses

Price: Free

Non-Profit
for non-profit organizations
  • - Up to 200 Employees
  • - Must be a registered non-profit organization
  • - Must be run through a verification process

Price: Free


Basic
for companies with fewer than 200 employees
  • - Up to 200 Employees
  • - Less than $500k in yearly revenue
  • - Ideal for businesses that want to scale

Price: $200/month

Enterprise
for companies with fewer than 1000 employees
  • - Up to 1000 Employees
  • - Less than $5m in yearly revenue
  • - Ideal for larger businesses

Price: $5000/month


Fortune 500
for companies with fewer than 5000 employees
  • - Up to 5000 Employees
  • - Less than $100m in yearly revenue
  • - Ideal for large corperations

Price: $10,000/month
